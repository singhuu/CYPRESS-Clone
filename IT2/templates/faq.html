Frequently Asked Questions

What is CYPRESS?

CYPRESS is a system that allows citizens to report problems regarding infrastructure that they notice on the street. Users can view, submit and track issue reports in the City of Toronto.


Who made CYPRESS?

The idea of CYPRESS was conceptualized by the City of Toronto. However, in 2021, under the leadership of esteemed Ryerson University Professor Vojislav B. Mišić and a group of very (very, very!) intelligent Ryerson students modernised CYPRESS for a course project.
